About The Author

CA Mukesh Mohan Gupta

Chartered Accountant | MSME Policy Expert | Author

Mukesh Mohan Gupta is a Chartered Accountant by Profession having more than 35 years of experience in Banking, Finance, MSME, Economy, Law Accounting and Auditing.

He has served the Board of Dena Bank as an Independent Director. He was appointed on the board of Artson Engineering Company (A subsidiary of TATA projects Limited), Hindustan fertilizers Limited, Madras Fertilizers Limited and JCT Electronics Limited by Board for Industrial and Financial Reconstruction (BIFR), Ministry of Finance, Government of India.

Currently, he serves as the President of the Chamber of Indian Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (CIMSME). He is also Member on the committees formed by various banks under the Framework for Revival and Rehabilitation of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises.

Mr. Gupta was also a member of the National Board of MSME, Ministry of MSME, Government of India.
